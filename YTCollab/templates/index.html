Let's Get Started:
Submit Channel ID

All we need in order to start recommending similar channels is your channel ID, a long serial starting with 'UC' that can be found on your advanced account page. Simply enter your ID and select a category you think your channel fits into, and we'll take care of the rest.

Example: UCxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx




How It Works

The recommender accesses an up-to-date DataFrame many of today's most successful YouTubers, separated by category. It then accesses the meta data of each channel's most recent videos to see which ones follow similar trends as you.




Why?

Often times, the best method of expanding a channel is to expand into an audience that is likely to appreciate your content, but it can be tricky sifting through channels to find the best fit for your brand of content. This tool was built to ease the search and give channels a solid starting point on their search for growth.
